// WHO WE ARE

About CJP

We are the world's only political party led by cockroaches, for cockroaches, and honestly — for humans too, whether they like it or not.

🪳

Our Origin

Founded in a kitchen cabinet behind the gas stove in 2024, CJP emerged when our founding cockroach, Comrade Kaalia, survived a Baygon attack and declared: "Enough is enough. We want representation."

🏛️

Our Vision

A nation where every citizen — human, cockroach, or rat — can steal food from the kitchen counter without fear of being squashed by a chappaal.

💪

Our Strength

We have survived nuclear tests, DDT sprays, and demonetisation. If that's not qualification enough to run a country, we don't know what is.

📊

Our Numbers

With an estimated 1,000,000,000 members in India alone (most of them living under your refrigerator), we are technically the largest political party in history.
